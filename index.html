ABOUT

The scale can be scary. Although we're smart enough to realize that the number on the scale doesn't tell the whole story, we can't but help feeling something when the numbers pop up. Whether the goal is lose or gain weight, the scale seems to be king in the measurement of progress.

If a picture is worth a thousand words, why do we trust the number on the scale?

This project was conceived of by Chithra Venkatesan in Jan 2015 for her final project in the Spring 2015 General Assembly Product Management course.
